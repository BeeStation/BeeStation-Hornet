Rukofamicom updated:

  • EMPs are now significantly less lethal to players with cybernetic implants, organs and limbs including IPCs. Most cybernetics have a maximum of a 30% chance to be affected by an EMP now, but it will be rolled separately for each limb/organ/implant so that the more you have the more likely you are to be affected in some way. This means IPCs will still be relatively weak to EMP, but crew can upgrade in moderation without fear of an EMP hitting them too hard.
  • Ion rifles and carbines have lost their AoE effect on the projectiles and now require a direct hit to trigger an EMP
  • Ion rifles have had their firing rate reduced from 2.5 shots per second to 1.5 shots per second
  • Ion rifles have had their capacity increased from 10 shots with a full charge to 15
  • Ion carbines are now fully automatic at 2.5 shots per second (this is the same fire rate they already had)
  • Ion carbines have a new capacity of 24, up from 10
  • Ion carbines no longer fit into bags
