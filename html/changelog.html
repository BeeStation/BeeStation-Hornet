Rukofamicom, Fikou updated:

  • Concussive gauntlets have arrived! Shaft miners will find that the diamond pickaxe can no longer be found in tendril chests, and instead they may find concussive gauntlets!
  • Tumor shards will now poll ghosts for control of lavaland elites if the original player has left the body. There is still no guarantee of having a new pet if alternatives are not available.

  • PKA modkits have been standardized to cost either 0%, 25%, or 50% capacity. This means it is now possible to utilize 100% of the weapon's potential and up to four impactful mods may now be installed!
  • PKA modkits that do AoE damage have had their power doubled, but still have an upper limit of 80% normal power. It is now far more practical to install AoE damage mods, but will still weaken the power of the weapon overall in exchange for versatility.
  • Damage increasing modkits have had their power halved. This was overlooked during the major lavaland rebalance that halved the HP of all fauna and halved the damage of mining tools. Damage modkits are supposed to increase damage by 25% of base power, not 50%.
  • All modkits are now compatible with mine bots, and the special cooldown reduction mod has been removed since the normal cooldown mod works on them now. Mine bots have the same capacity for mods as a PKA.
  • Explorer webbing can now hold additional mining tools and items: shovel, survival knife, emergency oxygen tank, PKA and plasma cutters. It can no longer hold kitchen knives.
  • Cleaned up some variable names and unnecessary code within the kinetic_accelerator file.
