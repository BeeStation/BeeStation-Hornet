You are a Giant Spider!

As a spider, following your directives are the most important.
If you have a master, follow their orders.

Spiders can vent crawl to traverse the station. Alt Click a vent to climb into it.
Webs block small-arms fire and slow down non-spiders, and most spiders can move a little more quickly on them. Spiders perform best by utilizing hit and run tactics to allow time for their slow-acting paralytic venom to work.

Each type of spider has different specialities and roles to fulfill
