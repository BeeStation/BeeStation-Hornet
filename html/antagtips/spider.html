You are a Giant Spider!

You are a Giant Spider, an eight-legged insect brought into existence on Space Station 13.

Spiders can vent crawl to traverse the station. Alt Click a vent to climb into it.

As a spider, your directives are the most important. If you have a master, follow their orders.

Midwife and Nurse spiders are capable of laying eggs and healing other spiders by attacking them. They can eat animals or humans to gain the ability to lay more eggs.

Guards and Hunters are more capable in combat than egg-laying spiders. Hunters are faster and have venom, whereas Guards are stronger and slower.

Eating humans will give egg-laying spiders the ability to lay enriched eggs. These eggs allow you to spawn one Viper, Tarantula, or Broodmother.

You are strongest in and around webs. Webs block small-arms fire and slow down non-spiders, and some spiders can move more quickly on them.
